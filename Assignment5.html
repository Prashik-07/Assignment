My Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. Sam Bahadur
    • Year: 2023
    • Main Actors: Vicky Kaushal, Fatima Sana Shaikh, Sanya Malhotra
    • Description: Based on the life of Sam Manekshaw, who was the Chief of the Army Staff of the Indian Army during the Indo-Pakistani War of 1971, and the first Indian Army officer to be promoted to the rank of field marshal.
  2. Uri-The Surgical Strike
    • Year: 2019
    • Main Actors:Vicky Kaushal, Yami Gautam
    • Description: The dramatized account of the retaliation to the 2016 Uri attack by Indian forces against terrorist camps based in POK. It is a rousing Indian patriotic film which gets your heart thumping. The movie got stunning cinematography, good dialogues and is well paced.
  3. Asoka
    • Year: 2001
    • Main Actors: Shah Rukh Khan, Kareena Kapoor
    • Description: This biopic set before his rise to power, young Prince Asoka works to perfect his skills in battle and also deals with family conflict. During a struggle with one of his step-brothers, his mother urges Asoka to escape to stay alive.
  4. Chhichhore
    • Year: 2019
    • Main Actors: Sushant Singh Rajput, Shraddha Kapoor
    • Description: A group of engineering students who go from being losers to choosers. The movie is told through flashbacks and takes place between the 1990s and the present day.
  5. Pippa
    • Year: 2023
    • Main Actors: Ishaan Khattar, Mrunal Thakur
    • Description: Tells the story of Captain Balram Singh Mehta of India's 45 Cavalry regiment who, along with his siblings, fought on the eastern front during the Indo-Pakistani war of 1971.
